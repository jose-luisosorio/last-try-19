My UX Skills

User Centered Design

I bring a deep understanding of empathizing with users and crafting intuitive experiences. Leveraging these insights, I strive to create products that resonate with users on a profound level. My expertise in User-Centered design allows me to stand out in the field by ensuring that every aspect of my work prioritizes user needs, resulting in impactful and user-friendly solutions.

Prototyping

By employing prototyping tools and techniques, I transform concepts into tangible experiences, facilitating clearer communication and stakeholder buy-in. This skill not only expedites the design cycle but also ensures that final products align closely with user expectations.

Wireframing

Through wireframing, I distill complex ideas into clear, navigable blueprints, fostering alignment among stakeholders and minimizing misunderstandings in the design process. My expertise in wireframing not only streamlines development timelines but also ensures that end products are intuitive and user-focused

My Work

User Research

Our team set out to provide a service that allows users to find a song based solely on their personal recollection, while using their own natural voice to search. Thus, eliminating the need to rush to ones phone, struggling to open an app in enough time, in hopes to perform a successful song search and match. Our team hopes to break new ground in the music discovery space that our predecessors have been unable to materialize thus far. As we undergo further research, we can become one of the most valuable tools in this next generation of AI-Assisted Music Exploration.

User Research

We are challenged with evaluating and improving the UI of a nonprofit. From initial brainstorming, we decided to explore improving the UI of the adoption process of the ASPCA website. We want to find out what pain points users might have during the process, and how we can push the UI design to help make adopting through the ASPCA website more efficient, effective, and enjoyable.

User Research

To this day, COVID-19 has undoubtedly changed the way travel and tourism operate. What was once a relatively easy process, has now become riddled with horror stories of non-refunded tickets, unexpected quarantines, and online ambiguity to international travel operations. My organization TrafalGar is developing a post-COVID flight planning app to help travelers find a way to navigate through the muddy waters of international travel in and out of post pandemic America.
